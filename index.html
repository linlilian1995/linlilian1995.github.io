19年风雨兼程，始终坚持价值投资，为投资者创造长期稳健回报 19 years of dedication to value investing, creating long-term steady returns for investors

深圳市翼虎投资管理有限公司成立于2006年11月，是中国领先的私募证券投资基金管理人之一。公司于2014年3月获得中国证券投资基金协会私募基金管理人资质，是国内首批50家阳光私募机构之一，亦是深圳地区最早成立的三家阳光私募之一，在行业内拥有深厚积淀与良好声誉。

Shenzhen Flying Tiger Investment Management Co., Ltd. was established in November 2006 and is one of China's leading private securities investment fund managers. The company obtained its private fund manager qualification from the China Securities Investment Fund Association in March 2014, making it one of the first 50 sunshine private funds in China and one of the earliest established in Shenzhen.

自2008年3月发行首只明星产品"中融信托·翼虎成长1期"以来，公司始终坚持长期价值投资理念，历经多轮牛熊转换，持续保持稳健的业绩表现。目前已成功发行并管理超过30只阳光私募产品，覆盖股票多头策略、指数增强策略、定投产品策略三大核心产品线。

Since the launch of our first flagship product "Zhongrong Trust · Flying Tiger Growth Phase 1" in March 2008, the company has adhered to long-term value investment philosophy, maintaining steady performance through multiple market cycles. We have successfully issued and managed over 30 sunshine private fund products, covering three core strategies: Stock Long-only, Index Enhancement, and Dollar-Cost Averaging.
